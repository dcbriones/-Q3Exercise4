Pokemon Center

Description:

Pokemon Centers in Japan are stores where people can buy authentic and original Pokemon merchandise.

What to do:

Choose from a wide variety of Pokemon merchandise to buy from such as Pokemon plushies, trading cards, small plastic figurines, and video games as well as take pictures with the decorations and afformentioned merchandise.
